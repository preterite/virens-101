### Illicium Arboretum Echoes (Locked)

**Decision:** Two echoes, matching Tsuga/Nyssa standard:
1. **Scent-before-sight** (Theoretical Framework, Trimbur paragraph): "reaching the writer before the text reaches the reader, the way a fragrance arrives before the substance that carries it" — echoes arboretum's "Star anise arrives in the nose before it greets the eye." ### Olea Arboretum Echoes (Locked)

**Decision:** Two echoes:
1. **Graft** (Theoretical Framework, Horner paragraph): "The graft that joins a wild rootstock to a cultivated variety produces fruit neither could produce alone, but the question of who harvests, who presses, and who trades remains open at every stage." Echoes arboretum's "You look for where the coppice begins: a swelling, a seam, a graft that cuts back and returns." Maps appropriation as structural relationship between rootstock and cultivar.

Echoes arboretum's olive regeneration. Literalizes the argument that institutional assessment operates on a representation without consuming the capital itself.

## Formulation Candidates

1. **[illicium/core-claim]** "Inscriptive durability and activating labor are the two conditions that make textual circulation possible: without durability, there is nothing to trace; without reading, there is nothing in motion." — Clean dyad establishing the page's structural argument.

2. **[illicium/reading-as-experience-good]** "Like generation, reading labor is an experience good: a text's value to its reader cannot be determined before the encounter and cannot be extracted without it. This makes reading the other experience good that Taxus identifies." — The "other experience good" formulation, developed from conversation history (2026-02-03).

3. **[olea/ontological-move]** "The claim is not that economic metaphors illuminate writing instruction. The claim is that writing instruction _is_ economic activity." — The cleanest statement of the webtext's central ontological argument.

4. **[olea/self-directed-leads]** "Self-directed appropriation is the form composition pedagogy most needs and least recognizes." — Positions the most disruptive form first.

5. **[olea/conditions-designable]** "The analysis is pedagogically actionable because the conditions are designable: if you can trace the internal structure of an appropriation form, you can redesign the conditions that sustain it." — Bridge from Resnick/Wolff theory to course design.

6. **[olea/sequence-is-distribution]** "The sequence is the distribution: by the time institutional assessment operates on the corpus, self-directed and gift forms have had fourteen weeks to build value that assessment cannot fully extract." — Constraint sequencing reframed as appropriation-distribution strategy.
