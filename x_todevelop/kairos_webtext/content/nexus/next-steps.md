## The Core Overlay / Syllogism / Cycle

Emphasize that this is a cycle: one can trace or enter this at any point and follow through the sequence.

- Dependency complexity reveals itself through the labor of engaging it as difficulty. (One encounters complexity by experiencing difficulty because complexity resists inspection.)
- Difficulty and dependency complexity are diachronic and synchronic instances of the same phenomenon. (Complexity describes structure as it exists in the moment; difficulty describes the encounter with that structure over time.)
- If complexity reveals itself only through engagement, then the value directly produced by that engagement is also only available through engagement. (The operations of difficulty as signal and value as experience good carry the same structure, in that both are unavailable until labor is performed.)
- Writing operates as a technology that converts the diachronic labor of difficulty into legible synchronic complexity. (Text captures part of the labor that revealed complexity and produced value, so that process becomes product, available for inspection and reuse, and experienced difficulty gets distilled into inspectable structure.)
- Constraint sequencing converts synchronic capital back into diachronic sequences manageable by the laboring agent. (Accumulated capital as index of past difficulty and value gets configured to permit the next encounter with complexity to fall within capacity.)
- Constraint sequencing creates kairotic conditions for directing accumulated capital toward new labor, which encounters new dependency complexity that reveals itself through engagement. (The opportune moment is where capital and labor meet and the encounter reopens the cycle.)
